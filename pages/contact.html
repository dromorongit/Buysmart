Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section, or by using the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with tags attached. Return shipping is free for defective items.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship to the United States, Canada, and select European countries. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.

How do I become a wholesale customer?

For wholesale inquiries, please contact our business development team at business@buysmart.com with details about your business and purchasing needs.

Are your products covered by warranty?

Most of our products come with manufacturer warranties. Warranty information is provided with each product, and we also offer extended warranty options for select items.
